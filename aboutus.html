About Eden

What is Eden?

Eden is an online platform designed to be a virtual haven for plant parents. It helps users manage their gardens, explore tips and tricks for plant care, and cultivate their green thumbs. The goal is to make gardening simple, enjoyable, and accessible for everyone, whether you're an experienced gardener or just starting out.

Challenges Faced

Developing Eden came with its challenges:

  • Ensuring the logo design and sizing worked seamlessly across all devices.
  • Making the site accessible and easy to navigate for plant enthusiasts of all skill levels.
  • Creating a cohesive, responsive design for both large and small screens.
  • Maintaining a consistent aesthetic to reflect the calming and nurturing nature of plants.
